ABOUT

Truman Olcott is a record producer, mixer, engineer, and musician. In his 30+ year career in the music industry, he has played countless shows, released numerous albums, and has written music with and for Grammy winners, Gold and Platinum record recipients, and Fortune 500 companies.

RECORD PRODUCTION

Owen will take you through all aspects of the record-making process. From demo review and song selection through tracking, editing and mixing, he will take your basic ideas and develop them into a professional, radio-ready EP or full-length album.

SONGWRITING

Whether you need help with lyrics, composition, arrangement, or all of the above, Owen can help you take your song from inspiration to reality.

PERFORMANCE

With experience including drums, acoustic, electric, and bass guitar, singing, and MIDI programming, Owen can add his tasteful and distinctive flair to your recording project.
